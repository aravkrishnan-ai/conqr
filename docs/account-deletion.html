Account Deletion

How to delete your Conqr account and data

Important: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed.

Option 1: Delete from the App

You can delete your account directly from within the Conqr app:

  1. Open the Conqr app
  2. Go to the Profile tab (bottom right)
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the page
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Your account and all associated data will be deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

  • Email: conqrrunning@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your Conqr account. We will process your request and delete your account within 30 days.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Account information - username, email, profile photo, bio
  • Activities - all recorded runs, walks, and rides
  • Territories - all claimed territories
  • Posts - all feed posts you created
  • Comments and likes - all your comments and likes on posts
  • Friend connections - all friendships and friend requests
  • Analytics data - all usage and analytics events
  • Reports and blocks - all reports you submitted and users you blocked

Note: After deletion, your username will no longer appear on the leaderboard, map, or in other users' friend lists.
